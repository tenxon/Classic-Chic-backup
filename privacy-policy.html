Privacy Policy

Classic Chic values your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you shop with us.

Information We Collect

We collect your name, email, phone number, shipping address, and payment details necessary to fulfil your orders.

How We Use Information

  • To process and deliver orders
  • To send order updates and receipts
  • To improve our website and services
  • To send marketing communications when you opt in

Data Security

Payment data is processed by PCI-DSS compliant providers. We never store card details on our servers.

Cookies

We use cookies for cart, wishlist, and personalisation. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by writing to care@classicchic.in.

Updates
