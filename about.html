Our Story

Craft, quietly.

Classic Chic is an atelier of Indian craftsmanship — silks, weaves, and hand-embroidery, tailored for the modern world.

Made by hand. Made to last.

Every piece we create begins on a loom in a workshop in India. Our weavers, embroiderers, and tailors carry generational skill — we simply protect the craft and pair it with modern silhouettes.

We believe premium clothing should be worn, not stored. So we build for daily elegance, whether you're at a wedding or a dinner across town.
